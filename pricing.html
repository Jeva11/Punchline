Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel at any time? ▾
Yes — cancel anytime from your account settings. No long-term contracts, no cancellation fees.
What happens to my content if I downgrade? ▾
Your content stays safe. If you exceed the free tier limits, older content will be archived (not deleted) until you upgrade again.
Do you take a cut of my merch or ticket sales? ▾
Pro plan has a 5% platform fee on merch sales. Enterprise plans can negotiate custom rates. We never take a cut of your subscription revenue.
Is there a free trial of paid plans? ▾
Yes! Creator and Pro plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.
